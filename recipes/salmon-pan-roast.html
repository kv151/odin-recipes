Salmon Pan Bake

Description

An easy weeknight dinner option, this recepie serves 4 or can optionally be meal prepped. Salmon is a great source of heatlhy fats and this recepie can be served with baked vegetables or on a bed of rice

Ingredients

  • 1kg salmon steak fillet
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2-3 heaped tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2-3 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp minced rosemary
  • 1 tbsp minced thyme
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Debone and slice the salmon into 4 even steaks
  2. Add 2tbsp butter to a pan on medium heat
  3. Add the panko breadcrumbs and toast until golden
  4. Add the rosemary and thyme to the panko and season to taste with salt and pepper then set aside to cool
  5. Combine equal parts Dijon mustard and mayonnaise in a bowl
  6. Place salmon on a lightly oiled baking sheet
  7. Generously brush the salmon with the mayonnaise and mustard mix
  8. Add one egg white to the panko mix and layer the mix onto the salmon
  9. Bake in a 160 Celcius oven for 20-25 minutes
  10. Let cool for 3 minutes then top the juice of a lemon slice
  11. Serve with baked vegetables or rice
