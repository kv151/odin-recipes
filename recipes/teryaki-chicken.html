Teriyaki Chicken

Description

A delicious and filling dish, this Japanese inspired meal prep contains stir fried chicken and vegetables coated in a home made Teriyaki sauce and served with a side of rice. This recepie makes 4 portions, which can be stored for up to 4 days in a fridge or 2-3 months in a freezer.

Ingredients

Stir fry ingredients

  • 1kg chicken breast fillets
  • 1 Large brown onion
  • 2 Carrots
  • 3 Bell peppers
  • 500g tenderstem brocolli
  • 1 large spring onion
  • 4 cups white rice
  • (Optional) chicken stock cube
  • (Optional) Seasame seeds

Teriyaki sauce ingredients

  • 300ml soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp seasame oil
  • 1 tsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 15g ginger
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tsp corn flour or corn starch

Steps

  1. Add 4 cups of washed white rice to a rice cooker, fill with water to indicated level for 4 cups and optionally add a cube of chicken stock.
  2. Finely slice one brown onion
  3. Finely slice 3 bell peppers
  4. Peel and slice carrots into thin strips. This can be done by slicing at an angle.
  5. Slice spring onions, separating the white root from the green stems
  6. Prepare the sauce by mixing all wet ingredients, grating the ginger, crushing the garlic and mixing together well
  7. Buttefly and dice the chicken breasts into even, bite-sized chunks
  8. Heat up 1tbsp of seasame oil in a pan and add the chicken over high heat
  9. When chicken is lightly browned (approx. 5 minutes), add the onion and bell peppers and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  10. Add the carrots and spring onion stems to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes
  11. Add in the sauce and mix together well, reducing the heat to medium-low
  12. Make a slurry with the corn flour by mixing with 100ml of water and add to the sauce to thicken
  13. Serve with the rice, garnishing with the spring onion greens and seasame seeds.
